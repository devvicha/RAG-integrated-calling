## Overview
Sampath Thilina is a gift voucher scheme that allows customers to save for gold jewellery purchases through regular monthly installments. It's an ideal way to accumulate savings for gold purchases over time.

## Key Benefits
- Systematic savings plan for gold jewellery purchases
- Monthly installment-based savings
- Secure and convenient way to save for gold
- No need for large upfront payments
- Can be used to purchase gold jewellery at participating outlets

## How It Works
1. Open a Sampath Thilina Gift Voucher account
2. Make regular monthly deposits
3. Accumulate savings over the chosen period
4. Use the accumulated amount to purchase gold jewellery

## Eligibility
- Available for Sri Lankan citizens above 18 years of age
- Valid identification document required (NIC, Passport, or Driving License)

## Features
- Flexible monthly installment amounts
- Fixed tenure options
- Gift voucher issued upon maturity
- Can be used at authorized jewellery outlets
- Safe and structured savings approach

## Documents Required
- Valid National Identity Card (NIC), Passport, or Driving License
- Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement within last 3 months)
- Initial deposit amount

## Usage
- Upon maturity, receive a gift voucher
- Present voucher at authorized jewellery outlets
- Purchase gold jewellery up to the voucher value
- Any excess amount can be paid separately

## Important Notes
- This is a savings product, not a loan facility
- Vouchers are valid for a specified period
- Check with branch for participating jewellery outlets
- Terms and conditions apply for early withdrawal
