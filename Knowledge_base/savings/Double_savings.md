## Overview
Sampath Double S is a savings account that rewards customers with a monthly bonus interest for maintaining a healthy account balance. It is designed to encourage consistent savings growth.

## What's Special
- Earn bonus interest calculated on the daily balance and credited monthly.
- To qualify for the bonus, an average balance of Rs. 15,000 per month must be maintained.
- Bonus interest will be paid for a maximum of one debit per interest cycle.

## Eligibility
Available to Sri Lankan citizens over 18 years of age with valid identification (NIC or Passport).  
A minimum initial deposit of Rs. 5,000 is required.

## Account Requirements
- Minimum average balance to earn interest: Rs. 2,000  
- Minimum average balance to avoid service charge: Rs. 1,000  
- Bonus interest will not be paid if more than one debit occurs per interest cycle.

## Branch Special Offers
- Bambalapitiya Branch: Minimum deposit Rs. 25,000  
- Colombo Super Branch: Minimum deposit Rs. 10,000

## Interest Calculation
The standard interest rate applies to all balances, plus bonus interest based on the tier above. Interest is calculated on the daily balance and credited monthly.

## Key Features
- Monthly bonus interest on qualifying balances
- Access to Sampath Vishwa internet banking
- Sampath Visa or Master Debit Card included
- ATM and CDM access across the island
- SMS alerts and e-statements available
